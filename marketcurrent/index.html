ABOUT US

MarketCurrents is a Research, Events and Marketing Company with a primary focus on trending industries. We are focused on promoting thought-leadership in a variety of formats and providing key market intelligence on the markets we serve. This is achieved through providing a platform of targeted roundtables (including customized event formats), as well as qualitative and quantitative reports. These mediums allow professionals engaged within those industries to come together, exchange as well as disseminate information. MarketCurrents has a global reach and a network of leading business professionals and companies which in turn offers your company unparalleled access.

MarketCurrents is separated into 3 business lines as follows:

RESEARCH

Produces specialized industry research reports that are circulated to a qualified audience that are composed of buyers and sellers within those markets.

EVENTS

ROUNDTABLES/MINIEVENTS

Brings together key decision makers and influencers in trending industries in a variety of meeting formats. Marketcurrents also produces bespoke, customized events for new companies that enter into a trending industry that we operate in.

MARKETING SERVICES

Provide branding, consultative and business development services for start-up companies, who are looking to enter new markets or for ways to identify new business opportunities.

RESEARCH & EVENTS

MarketCurrents Intelligence Unit is divided in 2 categories

MC-RESEARCH

Research

MarketCurrents produces a series of reports within the sectors it serves.

These reports are circulated to decision makers in their respective industries. They also include government agencies and scholars.

We typically employ data providers in producing these reports, who provide statistical analysis. Through a process of examining big data, organizations are able to identify patterns, trends and other useful intelligence insights. Our reports are specifically catered to be used by entrepreneurs, chief executives, chief financial officers as well as business analysts to examine the potential growth and pitfalls of their markets.



MC-EVENTS

MarketCurrents organize trade events and other live ‘in person’ events which help businesses to do business, transact, learn and network as part of a professional or commercial community

Roundtables & Events

MarketCurrents produce high-quality roundtables which are attended by senior level executives.

Given our focus of specializing in emerging industries, they in turn, come with ever-changing regulations, products, technologies, opportunities and risks. Providing a forum/platform to keep abreast of these changes is essential.

Through electronic and face to face platforms MarketCurrents connects people to foster new relationships, partnerships and discuss the latest currents in their markets.

When industries and companies are in their infancy stage, events and other face to face meeting formats are critical to enable solid relationships to be forged.
